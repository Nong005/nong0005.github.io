During my formative years, I developed a passion for drawing and painting. At 13th, I earn recognition at both State and National levels, including Lions Peace Poster Contest organised by Lions Club of International. Hailing from Manipur, India, I aspire to merge my artistic talents with modern technology, now pursuing IMD at Algonquin College, Canada. The courses here are truly inspiring, making me optimistic about realizing my dreams.

Beyond my artistic pursuits, I am an avid explorer of cultures and cuisines. Traveling has opened my eyes to diverse traditions and flavours, enriching my understanding of the world. Whether savoring traditional Emrati Cusine in the bustling markets of Dubai or admiring the breathtaking beauty of Niagara Falls, each experience fuels my curosity and appreciation for life's wonders.
