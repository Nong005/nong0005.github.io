About Us

Welcome to Sanatombi Oriental English School, a beacon of educational excellence since 1980. Managed by a dedicated team, our institution stands tall in the heart of Manipur, offering quality education in an urban setting. Catering to grades 1 through 10, our co-educational environment fosters holistic development, with a pre-primary section nurturing young minds for a bright future. With English as our medium of instruction, we ensure students receive a globally competitive education, preparing them for success in an ever-evolving world. Conveniently accessible via all-weather roads, our school opens its doors every April, igniting the academic journey of countless learners.

Our Team

N.Shantakumar Singh

Founder, SOES

The founder of our school, Nongmaithem Shantakumar Singh, embarked on a remarkable journey of service and dedication from humble beginnings. Despite the challenges of the 90s, marked by the scarcity of modern conveniences, he emerged as a tireless advocate for his community. His leadership roles in various civil organizations underscore his commitment to societal welfare, pioneering initiatives from healthcare to youth empowerment. Recognized for his contributions, he earned the District Youth Award in 1995, a testament to his unwavering spirit. Despite setbacks, including misinformation that thwarted his national recognition, his resolve only strengthened. His tenacity culminated in a triumphant moment, as he received the long-awaited honor at the National Youth Festival. Today, as the founder of our school, his ethos of perseverance continues to inspire us, shaping our commitment to excellence and service.

Our esteemed principal, N. Angouba Singh, epitomizes a legacy of leadership and dedication to education.
